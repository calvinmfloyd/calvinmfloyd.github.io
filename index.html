ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Calvin Floyd! Most people call me Cal. I have a passion for problem solving and utilizing data to optimize decision making. I'm always searching for ways to become a more well-rounded data scientist.

Currently, I'm a Senior Data Scientist working for the Sacramento Kings. Before this, I received a bachelors degree in mathematics from SUNY Geneseo and a masters degree in Applied and Computational Mathematics from Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). RIT's College of Science actually did a profile on me in 2021!

My main hobbies include video games, puzzles, TV shows, movies and both playing and watching sports. I've played basketball my entire life and, growing up in upstate NY, I'm a huge fan of Syracuse Orange basketball and the Buffalo Bills. Overall, I like to try out new things to see if they stick or not.
